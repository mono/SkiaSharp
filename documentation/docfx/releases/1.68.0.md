## Highlights

SkiaSharp 1.68 is a CI-focused refresh that shipped in a single preview off 1.60.3. The Windows binaries are now statically linked, and the Linux and Groovy-scripted CI paths were reworked to make pipelines faster and more reliable. HarfBuzzSharp 1.4.6.2 ships alongside this release; see the [linked page](harfbuzzsharp/1.4.6.2.md) for its binding details.
