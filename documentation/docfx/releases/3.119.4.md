## Highlights

The first stable release of the 3.119.x line. Rolls up all 34 PRs from 3.119.3 (preview only, never released as stable) plus 101 PRs from Preview 1 of 3.119.4 plus 5 "unreleased" PRs — 140 PRs total against the 3.119.2 baseline. Ships alongside **[HarfBuzzSharp 8.3.1.5](harfbuzzsharp/8.3.1.5.md)** — see that page for its binding details.

Two big themes carry the release: **infrastructure and AI-assisted workflows** (a full slate of AI skills for triage, reproduction, bug-fix, native dependency update, Skia update, review, release, and sample validation; new bot pipelines; and a redesigned docs/gallery site), and **native modernization** (libpng to 1.6.58, libwebp to 1.6.0, libexpat to 2.7.3, brotli to 1.2.0, plus Linux ARM64 fontconfig fixes and Spectre mitigation flags from [@sshumakov](https://github.com/sshumakov)). Also lands `SkiaSharp.Views.Gtk4` using GirCore ([@Copilot](https://github.com/apps/copilot-swe-agent)), the Blazor WASM Gallery redesign, Skottie support surface additions, a `SKManagedStream` refactor to snapshot via `SKData` ([@Copilot](https://github.com/apps/copilot-swe-agent)), MSIX packaging for WinUI, delay-loading of D3D12 DLLs, .NET 10 support, Linux Bionic native assets ([@4Darmygeometry](https://github.com/4Darmygeometry)), a `net48` full-framework target ([@4Darmygeometry](https://github.com/4Darmygeometry)), and an AoT-crash fix when `IlcDisableReflection=true` ([@Copilot](https://github.com/apps/copilot-swe-agent)).
