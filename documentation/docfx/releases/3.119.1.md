# Version 3.119.1

> **Bugfix & maintenance release** ┬Ę Released September 23, 2025 ┬Ę [NuGet](https://www.nuget.org/packages/SkiaSharp/3.119.1)

## Highlights

A stability-focused release that fixes several crashes and platform-specific issues. Metal rendering on Apple platforms received the most attention ŌĆö double-initialization and iOS 15 compatibility are both resolved. Windows users benefit from a fix for WinUI loading in unpackaged apps, and a new `PostScriptName` property lands on `SKTypeface`. Four community contributors drove the majority of the user-facing fixes.

*None in this release.*
