## Highlights

HarfBuzzSharp 8.3.0 ships alongside SkiaSharp 3.0.0 and reflects the version-alignment jump made during the 3.0 modernization. It picks up an update to HarfBuzz 7.3.0, moves to .NET 8.0 and function-pointer/`LibraryImport` interop, removes obsolete members and types, and drops the older API-scan wiring in favor of the new post-pack path.

## Breaking Changes

Some obsolete HarfBuzzSharp members and types were removed as part of the 3.0 cleanup. See the [breaking-changes diff](8.3.0/HarfBuzzSharp/HarfBuzzSharp.breaking.md) for the exact signatures.
