## Highlights

SkiaSharp 3.116.0 is the first stable release of the 3.x line, cumulatively rolling up every preview cut that appeared under the [3.0.0](3.0.0.md) tag (which was preview-only and never shipped as its own stable NuGet). On top of the [3.0.0](3.0.0.md) work — Skia m116 uplift, `SKGLView`, WinUI accelerated views, WPF `SKGLElement`, Skottie's `Animation.Builder`, `SKImage.ToRawShader`, Metal-backed Mac Catalyst, `SKBlender`, .NET 8/9 support, and the full `[Obsolete]` cleanup — this release also adds Preview 6 additions (multi-thread/SIMD WebAssembly, `SKMatrix.MapPoints`/`SKMatrix.MapVectors`, `SKPath.AddPoly` Span overloads) and Blazor/WASM fixes. HarfBuzzSharp 8.3.0 ships alongside this release; see the [linked page](harfbuzzsharp/8.3.0.md) for its binding details.

## Breaking Changes

The cumulative rollup carries every breaking change from the [3.0.0](3.0.0.md) preview line — the `[Obsolete]` removals, SkiaSharp.Views reshaping, and Windows/desktop renames. See the [breaking-changes diff](3.116.0/SkiaSharp/SkiaSharp.breaking.md), the [SkiaSharp.Views diffs](3.116.0/SkiaSharp.Views/SkiaSharp.Views.iOS.breaking.md) (Android, Mac, Tizen, iOS, tvOS), and the [Blazor](3.116.0/SkiaSharp.Views.Blazor/SkiaSharp.Views.Blazor.breaking.md), [Maui.Controls](3.116.0/SkiaSharp.Views.Maui.Controls/SkiaSharp.Views.Maui.Controls.breaking.md), [WinUI](3.116.0/SkiaSharp.Views.WinUI/SkiaSharp.Views.Windows.breaking.md), [Uno.WinUI](3.116.0/SkiaSharp.Views.Uno.WinUI/SkiaSharp.Views.Windows.breaking.md), [Desktop.Common](3.116.0/SkiaSharp.Views.Desktop.Common/SkiaSharp.Views.Desktop.Common.breaking.md), and [WindowsForms](3.116.0/SkiaSharp.Views.WindowsForms/SkiaSharp.Views.WindowsForms.breaking.md) diffs for the exact list.
