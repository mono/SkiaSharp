## Highlights (rolled up into 4.148.0)

- **Skia m147** upgrade, contributed by [@ramezgerges](https://github.com/ramezgerges) ([#3702](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/3702)); Skia m148 lands later as part of the 4.148.0 rollup.

- **Variable font support** for SkiaSharp and HarfBuzzSharp, contributed by [@ramezgerges](https://github.com/ramezgerges) ([#3703](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/3703)); **color font palette** support and improved variable-font robustness ([#3742](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/3742)).

- **Animated WebP encoding** (`SKWebpEncoder`) ([#3771](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/3771)).
- **`HBColor` struct** for a proper `hb_color_t` representation ([#4000](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/4000)).
- **`SKSurface.Draw` and `SKCanvas.DrawSurface` `SKSamplingOptions` overloads**, contributed by [@Copilot](https://github.com/apps/copilot-swe-agent) ([#3491](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/3491)).
- **Tizen SDK 10** ([#3743](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/3743)), **freetype 2.14.3** ([#3726](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/3726)), **libjpeg-turbo 3.1.4.1** ([#4012](https://github.com/mono/SkiaSharp/pull/4012)).
- Numerous fixes contributed by [@ramezgerges](https://github.com/ramezgerges), [@sasakrsmanovic](https://github.com/sasakrsmanovic), [@ebariche](https://github.com/ebariche) (SkiaFiddle sample), [@SimonvBez](https://github.com/SimonvBez), [@4Darmygeometry](https://github.com/4Darmygeometry), and [@Copilot](https://github.com/apps/copilot-swe-agent).
